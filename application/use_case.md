# Use Case: Emergency Delivery

The drone can be used to deliver small essential items in short distances.

## Example scenarios
- Delivering medicine in hostels
- Sending tools in workshops
- Quick transport in restricted areas

## Advantages
- Faster than walking
- Can reach difficult areas
- Reduces manual effort

---

## Observation

Adding payload affects:
- Stability
- Motor load
- Battery consumption

Proper balancing is required.
